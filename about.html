LCHF (Low Carbohydrate High Fat) Diet


60% of Indian population, above the age of 40 years suffer from one or two metabolic disorders.

Incidence of metabolic syndrome increased in recent times.

These diseases are Obesity, Diabetes,Elivated BP, Heart diseas, Cancer, PCOD, Thyroid, Arthritis etc..

Insulin resistance is strongly associated with these diseases.

Many of these disorders can be reversed or controlled without medicines.

Simple life style changes and particulary change of diet is helpful to control many of these diseases.

Diet alters the harmone levels in the body, particulary Insulin .

Intermittent fasting and exercise enhance LCHF(low carbohyrate high fat diet) program to achieve better outcomes in shorter period.

Seven pathways for life style modification
Diet, Fasting, Exercise are essential
